## Customize your brain to fit your needs.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Model" icon="robot">
    For each of your brains, you can choose which model you want to use.
</Card>

<Card title="Max Token" icon="paragraph">
  You can set the maximum token count to define the maximum size of your brain's responses.
</Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  You can also update the name, description and snippet of the brain if needed.
</Tip>

## Give instructions to your brain

The instructions you give to a brain guide the system to perform specific
tasks or generate targeted responses. Well-crafted prompts help the AI
understand your needs and deliver accurate, relevant results.
